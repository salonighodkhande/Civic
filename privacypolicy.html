CONTENT

The Buldhana Municipal Corporation (Citizen Grievance Redressal System) is a web-based enterprise solution that enables the Municipal Corporation to enhance citizen satisfaction through comprehensive service management and efficient service delivery.

Buldhana Municipal Corporation automates the entire complaint process right from registration to closure. It enforces service level policies to ensure that the complaint is attended to by the right department, within the desired timeline, and escalated to higher authorities for their attention and intervention in case of non-compliance with service level/quality. Once a complaint is registered in the system, it is assigned to the concerned area officer dealing with the reported problem. The system automatically sends an SMS to the officer, alerting them about the complaint and prompting the required actions.


The officer is expected to resolve the complaint within a specified period. Once a complaint is resolved, the officer marks it as closed in the system. The citizen then receives an SMS confirming the resolution of the complaint. If the citizen is not satisfied, he/she can request to re-open the complaint, which will then be escalated to the higher authority.
