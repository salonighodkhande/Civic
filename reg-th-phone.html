📞 Register Complaint via Phone

Dial our toll-free helpline 1800-570-1235 anytime to register your complaint. Our operators are available 24x7 and will verify your details to ensure quick resolution. Please keep your information handy for faster support.

✉ Register Complaint via Email

Send us a mail at support@complaintportal.gov with your Name, Contact No., and Complaint Details. Our team will validate the information and register your complaint in the system. In case of incomplete details, one of our officials will reach out to you directly.

💬 Register Complaint via WhatsApp

Message "Hi" on WhatsApp to +91 8141231235 and choose Register Complaint from the menu. Our chatbot will guide you step-by-step through the process and confirm once your complaint is registered in the system.
